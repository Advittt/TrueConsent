# TrueConsent — Pitch Script

## Hook

> "1 in 5 insurance claims gets denied. Less than 1% of people appeal —
> even though 7 in 10 'not medically necessary' denials get overturned when they do.
> That's $265 billion sitting on the table. We built TrueConsent to pick it up."

---

## The Problem

- **1 in 5** claims denied by US insurers (KFF, 2024)
- **< 1%** of patients ever file a formal appeal
- **70%** of "not medically necessary" denials are overturned when challenged (Healthcare Dive)
- **$265 billion** spent annually by hospitals and patients fighting denials (Aptarro, 2026)
- **$18 billion** wasted arguing over denials that were ultimately paid anyway
- **$20 billion/year** is the provider-side cost to contest denials alone (AHA)

The system is designed to exhaust you. Denials are written in code — CARC CO-97, ICD-10 K80.20, CPT 47562 — language no patient is expected to understand.

---

## The Solution

TrueConsent turns a 60-page EOB into a complete appeal packet in under 60 seconds.

**Step 1 — Upload**
Patient drops their Explanation of Benefits (PDF, image, or text).

**Step 2 — Decode**
We extract every CPT code, ICD-10 diagnosis, and CARC denial reason. We cross-reference our code database and flag every wrongful denial with a case strength score.

**Step 3 — Appeal Letter**
We generate a medically precise, legally grounded appeal letter — citing policy, statute, and precedent. It reads like it was written by a billing attorney.

**Step 4 — Call**
Our AI agent calls the insurer's provider relations line, navigates the IVR, presents the appeal, and gets a reference number — while the patient watches a live transcript.
